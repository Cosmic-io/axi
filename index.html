Frequently
Asked Questions

Design, development, and automation without the complexity.

What should I expect during my demo call?

During your demo call, we'll walk through your current challenges, goals, and how AXI can help. We'll show you relevant examples of our work and outline a recommended approach for your project. No pressure — just a conversation to see if we're a good fit.

How fast will I receive my deliverables?

Most requests are completed within 2-5 business days. More complex projects like full websites or applications may take 1-3 weeks depending on scope.

What if I don't like the design?

We'll keep revising until you're 100% satisfied. We work iteratively with frequent check-ins to ensure we're aligned every step of the way.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can make?

No limit on requests. We work through them one at a time based on your active slot. You can queue as many as you like and we'll prioritize them together.

How does the AI automation work?

We build custom AI workflows using tools like OpenAI, Claude, and Make.com to automate repetitive tasks in your business — from lead qualification to content generation to internal operations.

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes. You can pause your subscription at any time and resume when you're ready. Your remaining days carry over so you never lose what you've paid for.
